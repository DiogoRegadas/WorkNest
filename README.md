WorkNest is an **academic full-stack project** developed as the final assignment of my **BSc in Computer Science and Engineering**.  
It is a **collaboration and project management platform** designed to centralize multiple productivity functionalities into a single space ‚Äî helping reduce **digital fatigue** caused by constant switching between scattered tools (ex.: Trello, Slack, Google Docs, Notion, etc.)

Designed with a strong focus on **functional centralization**, usability, and modern development practices.
